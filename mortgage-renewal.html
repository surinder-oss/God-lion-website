Mortgage Renewal

Renew Smarter,
Not Just Again.

Your lender will send you a renewal notice 120 days before your term ends. But you don't have to accept their offer. We help Calgary homeowners shop for better rates, renegotiate terms, and avoid leaving thousands on the table when renewal time arrives.

Mortgage Renewals
That Maximize Savings.

Your lender knows you're busy. They hope you'll sign their renewal offer without shopping around. But a 0.5% rate difference on a $500,000 mortgage means real money in your pocket. We handle the shopping, the negotiation, and the paperwork so you get the best deal available.

Our Approach

  • Rate shopping across 200+ lenders — we find options your current lender won't mention
  • Renewal analysis 120 days before term end — strategic timing to maximize leverage
  • Lender comparison and negotiation — we push for better rates and terms on your behalf
  • Switch or stay analysis — clear guidance on whether switching or negotiating makes sense
  • Seamless processing — we handle all coordination so you just sign at closing
