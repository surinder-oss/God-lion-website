What's Inside

Every Tool, Explained

Here's exactly what each tool does — and why it matters before you apply.

01
Pre-Qualification

Find Out If You Qualify — Before Applying

Get pre-qualified in minutes without triggering a hard credit inquiry. See your estimated mortgage amount based on your income, debts, and down payment.

No credit check — your score stays untouched
Enter income and debt details manually
Get an estimated approval range instantly
Share results with your realtor or advisor
02
Affordability Calculator

Know Your Maximum Purchase Price

Enter your household income, monthly debts, and available down payment — the app calculates the maximum home price and mortgage you qualify for.

Uses current GDS/TDS ratios used by lenders
Adjusts automatically for CMHC insurance thresholds
Generates a report you can bring to showings
Works for single or joint incomes
03
Closing Cost Estimator

No Surprises on Closing Day

Closing costs catch a lot of first-time buyers off guard. This tool estimates every cost upfront — specific to Alberta — so you know exactly how much cash you need.

Land title transfer fees (Alberta-specific)
Legal fees and disbursements
Home inspection, appraisal, and title insurance estimates
Available rebates and first-time buyer credits applied
04
Mortgage Stress Test

See If You Pass Before the Bank Checks

Canada's federal stress test requires you to qualify at a higher rate than you'll actually pay. Run the test yourself before any lender does — no surprises.

Uses the current federal qualifying rate (5.25% or contract + 2%)
Shows your maximum borrowing power under stress test rules
Compares qualifying rate vs your actual payment
Identifies the gap if you don't currently qualify
05
Rate Comparison

Compare 100+ Lenders Side by Side

Don't settle for your bank's posted rate. Search and compare live mortgage rates from banks, credit unions, monolines, and alternative lenders — all in one place.

Real rates from 100+ Canadian lenders
Filter by fixed vs variable, term length, and type
See payment impact side by side for each rate
One tap to connect with Surinderpal for any rate
06
Renewal & Refinance

See What You Could Save at Renewal

Mortgage coming up for renewal? Compare what your bank is offering against what the market has available. The savings often surprise people.

Enter your current mortgage balance and rate
Compare against current best available rates
See monthly and total savings over the new term
Includes penalty estimates for early switch scenarios
07
Down Payment Calculator

Know the Minimum Before You Start Saving

Canadian down payment rules are more nuanced than most people think. This tool calculates the exact minimum based on the purchase price — and when CMHC insurance kicks in.

5% minimum for homes under $500K
Graduated rules for the $500K–$999K range
20% threshold to avoid CMHC insurance
Shows CMHC premium added to mortgage if applicable
08
PDF Summary Reports

Email Your Results as a Professional PDF

Every calculation in the app can be exported as a clean, formatted PDF summary — ready to share with your realtor, accountant, or financial advisor.

One tap to email any calculation as a PDF
Includes Surinderpal's contact info on every report
Clean, professional format — ready to share
Useful for pre-offer conversations with realtors
