Investment Mortgages
That Make Sense.

Banks have strict rules about rental income verification and debt ratios. Investor clients need lenders who understand that your first property is your foundation, and your second is your growth strategy. We use rental income, expense analysis, and property-specific underwriting to get you approved.

Our Approach

  • Rental income verification — we document and present your cash flow to maximize qualification
  • Debt service ratio optimization — structured analysis to show lenders your actual carrying capacity
  • Multi-property portfolios — financing for 2nd, 3rd, and portfolio expansion
  • Cash flow analysis — understand profitability before committing to the investment
  • Investor-friendly lenders — access to B-lenders and private options for more flexibility
