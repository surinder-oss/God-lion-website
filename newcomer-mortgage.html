Newcomer Mortgage

Welcome Home
In Canada.

Starting your life in a new country is brave. Buying your first home here shouldn't be complicated. We specialize in mortgages for newcomers to Canada — we understand the documentation challenges and know which lenders embrace recent immigrants.

Newcomer Mortgages
That Actually Work.

Your income history might not fit traditional lending boxes. You might not have a Canadian credit history yet. But your ambition and financial stability are real. We know how to present your application so lenders see your potential, not your timeline in Canada.

Our Approach

  • Flexible documentation — we accept international credentials and income verification
  • Newcomer-friendly lenders — access to programs designed for recent immigrants
  • Credit building strategies — establish Canadian credit while qualifying for your mortgage
  • Multi-year income averaging — we don't penalize you for recent arrival
  • International experience recognition — your work history counts, even if it's not Canadian
