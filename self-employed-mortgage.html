Self-Employed Mortgage

Your Business Income
Counts Here.

Being self-employed means your income doesn't fit in a box. We know how to present your business finances to lenders so you qualify for the mortgage your business deserves.

Self-Employed Mortgages
That Actually Work.

Traditional income verification doesn't capture what you actually earn. Banks see NOAs and tax returns and miss the full picture of your business. We see differently. We use stated income programs, bank statement qualifying, and access to specialized lenders who understand self-employed income.

Our Approach

  • Stated income programs for those with significant business write-offs
  • Bank statement qualifying — we use your actual cash flow, not just your NOA
  • Access to B-lenders and private lenders who specialize in self-employed clients
  • Strategies to protect your tax efficiency while still qualifying
  • Competitive rates — self-employed doesn't mean you pay a penalty
